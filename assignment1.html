My Summer Adventures

My Trip to California

It was a long,quick trip.We loaded our vechile and headed to northern California for the 4th of July weekend for a family reunion. We then made haste to magic Mountain.By the time we returned home, we had traveled some 1,300 miles in three and one half days.

My Trip To The Rockies

After a year's preparation, it was time to go backpacking with the Boy Soouts, we flew to colorado Springs,tourned the Air Force Academy,Pike's Peak, Garden Of The Gods, and the Olympic training center. We chartered a bus to the Philmont Scout Ranch in Northern New Mexico an began our 60 mile backpack trek.Afterwards, we boarded Amtrack's and rode the rails to kingman, and then chartered a bus home.

My Trip To The Mid West

My wife and I drove our son's little red Ford Mustang some 2,900 miles to him in Indiana where he attends college.In route,we stopped at Nauvoo,Illinois,and Detriot, Michigan. We then boared Amtrak in Chicago, and rode it to Salt Lake City, where we boarded a bus which returned us to Las Vegas

Concluding Thought About The Summer

What A  Summer  If only they could all be this great!
